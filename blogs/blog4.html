Welcome To Professional Premium Dry Cleaning Service

Shekhar (2023 4 October)

Cleaning is the process of removing dirt, dust, stains, and odors using liquids, powders, and sprays. Additionally, we provide a variety of top-notch dry cleaning services in Delhi, including clothing washing, carpet cleaning, lagoon wet cleaning, and dry cleaning of clothing. We always use the best chemicals to provide the best cleaning services. It is critical to us that you are happy with our cleaning services, which is why we provide the best dry cleaning service possible.

Our dry cleaning procedure begins with the preparation of clothing using spotting and color treatment techniques. We utilize perchloroethylene, more often referred to as perc, in our dry cleaning equipment to ensure the best possible care for the garments we clean. This technique does not utilize water and the cleaning chemicals do not permeate the fabric, which is why it is unique and best suited for delicate fabric clothes.

Extremely delicate clothing is dry cleaned using a hydrocarbon machine, which involves a number of processes, including washing with the hydrocarbon solvent, cleaning, drying with heat, and finally chilling the garment to prevent shrinkage.
